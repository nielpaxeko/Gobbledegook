FAQ'S

Want to Know More? Discover How Blink Works!

Once you have a membership with Blink Enterprises you'll first need to head towards one of our terminals. From there, you'll be able to select a location from the various other terminals around the world as long as they are in range of your membership plan. You'll then be teleported to your chosen location in the blink of an eye!

Blink's public terminals are strategically located in major cities worldwide to ensure maximum accessibility. You can find our terminals in cities such as New York, London, Tokyo, Paris, Sydney, and many more. For a complete list of locations, please visit the "Locations" section on our website.

When you visit a Blink terminal, you'll need to bring a valid form of identification and your Blink membership card. Ensure your membership details are up to date in your Blink account. For international travel, please bring any required travel permits.

All of our locations have several blink stations. Up to 10 people can enter a station at a time. Note that all people that enter the same station will be sent to the same location.

Once you have confirmed your destination at the terminal, changes can only be made before you step into the teleport machine. If you need to change your destination, please inform the staff immediately. Note that changes may result in a short delay while we reconfigure your teleportation details.

You are allowed to bring one carry-on item per passenger when using our Blink terminals. Ensure that your carry-on complies with our size and weight restrictions, which can be found in your Blink account details. Please note that illegal items, hazardous materials, and any substances prohibited by local or international law are strictly forbidden. Additional fees apply for extra items or oversized luggage. Always check the specific regulations for your destination before traveling.
